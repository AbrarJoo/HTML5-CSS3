Breaking: Local Cat Becomes Mayor Overnight

Town Votes Unanimously After Viral “Nap Campaign” Wins Hearts

By Alex Smith

In a surprising turn of events, a sleepy orange cat named Muffin has been elected mayor of a small town after a viral video showcased his “nap-first, stress-later” campaign strategy. Residents, charmed by Muffin’s calm demeanor and refusal to engage in political debates, rallied behind the unlikely candidate.

On election day, voters overwhelmingly chose Muffin, citing his ability to “listen quietly” and “do absolutely nothing wrong.” While officials scramble to understand the legal implications, Muffin has already settled into his new role—curled up on the mayor’s chair, blissfully unaware of his landslide victory.
