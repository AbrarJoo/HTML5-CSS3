The Devil's Advocate

The Devil's Advocate (1997) is a stylish, provocative legal thriller blending supernatural horror with themes of vanity and unchecked ambition, elevated by Al Pacino’s magnetic, theatrical performance as Satan. Keanu Reeves delivers a solid performance as a morally conflicted defense attorney, while Charlize Theron provides a heartbreaking, emotional core as his unraveling wife. While sometimes over-the-top, the film excels in tension and atmosphere, delivering a memorable exploration of temptation.
