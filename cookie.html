Cookies

this is a simple cookie recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and cream 150g softened butter, 80g sugar, and 80g brown sugar together in a bowl.
  2. Beat in 1 large egg and 2 tsp vanilla extract until the mixture is creamy.
  3. Mix in 225g plain flour, 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda, and 1/4 tsp salt until a dough forms, then stir in 200g chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes until golden brown.
